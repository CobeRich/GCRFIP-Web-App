# GCRFIP Official Website Copy Deck

## Purpose
This copy deck provides the approved narrative and messaging structure for core website pages.
The goal is consistency of voice, strategic clarity, and clear calls to action.

## Voice Principles
- Strategic and confident
- National in scope, community in impact
- Evidence-led and action-oriented
- Inclusive of government, academia, civil society, and industry

## Core Narrative Spine
1. Ghana faces an escalating national flood resilience challenge.
2. Legacy response models are not sufficient for future risk.
3. GCRFIP provides an integrated predictive intelligence model.
4. The six pillars operationalize resilience at national scale.
5. Measurable social and economic impact is achievable.
6. Stakeholders can engage through partnership, research, and delivery.

## Editorial Consistency Rules
- Prefer short strategic statements over long paragraphs
- Keep sentence openings active and direct
- Use measurable framing where possible
- Keep CTA verbs explicit and consistent
- Avoid mixing informal and institutional tone in the same section
